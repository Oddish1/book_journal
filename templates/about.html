About BookJournal

BookJournal is a web application designed to help readers track, reflect on, and share their reading experiences. Whether you're a casual reader or a dedicated book lover, BookJournal provides a space to document your journey through literature.

What You Can Do

  • Search and Save Books: Use the integrated Google Books API to discover and save books to your personal library.
  • Track Your Reading: Log your progress with journal entries, noting the page you're on, your thoughts, and reflections.
  • Write Reviews: Share your opinion with the community by writing and rating books you've read.
  • Discover Through Others: Browse reviews from other readers to find new titles or see what others think about a book you're considering.
  • Organize with Lists: Keep track of books you want to read, are currently reading, or have finished.

Why BookJournal?

BookJournal was created to combine the simplicity of personal journaling with the power of community-driven recommendations. Instead of just tracking what you’ve read, BookJournal encourages thoughtful reflection—capturing not just what you read, but how it made you feel.

Built With

BookJournal is developed using the Django web framework with a SQLite database backend. It also utilizes:

  • Google Books API for book metadata
  • FontAwesome for intuitive icons
  • A responsive, custom CSS design for accessibility across devices
